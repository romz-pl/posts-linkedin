# OpenFOAM: The Open-Source CFD Code


**OpenFOAM** is the leading software for computational fluid dynamics (CFD). It is written in C++ (1.5 million lines), is free and open source, and has a user base spanning industry, government, research, and academia worldwide.

**OpenFOAM** is primarily used for research into new technologies, product design and optimization, safety calculations, and problem troubleshooting. OpenFOAM calculates fluid motion and forces, heat transfer, thermodynamics, and chemistry in fluids and solids as an alternative to experiments, providing greater detail and accuracy than approximate calculation methods.

**OpenFOAM** is an alternative to proprietary CFD software, whose license fees are comparable to the payroll costs of each CFD engineer. This effectively doubles the direct expenses associated with CFD. OpenFOAM enables faster innovation by providing the freedom to customize the source code, automate calculations, and collaborate with partners. OpenFOAM eliminates the risks of vendor lock-in and outgrowing a restricted, proprietary platform.
